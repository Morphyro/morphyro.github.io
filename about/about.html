About

The Surrealist Inner Society is a group of explorers, researchers, developers, artists, and thinkers who seek to explore the unconscious mind and the irrational aspects of reality. We believe that by accessing the surreal, we can unlock new ways of seeing and understanding the world around us. Through a variety of mediums, including machine learning, neural networks, natural language processing, deep learning, artificial intelligence, art, writing, and performance, we seek to challenge conventional ways of thinking and push the boundaries of human consciousness for the advancement of social well-being.
